About Us

We are real passionate lovers and collectors, and that is very important to us. Our objective is that our customers are satisfied by their acquisitions and by the service which we bring to them. We select in priority artists who already have an artistic carrier. Young talented artists are of course also welcome, they differ by their technique and quality of their productions. Besides, Sold Art is not just a “virtual” art gallery. We like meeting our customers, our artists, our partners because human contact is part of our values.
